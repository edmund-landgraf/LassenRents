Field operations

Dispatch and driver flow

Dispatch turns rentals and pickups into scheduled jobs. Inspection is a master-detail screen where drivers work one job at a time against a shared five-step checklist.

Operational flow

  1. Review unassigned dropoff, pickup, and service jobs on the dispatch board.
  2. Match the job to truck capability such as 20 ft, 40 ft, and high-cube hauling.
  3. Advance the job from Unassigned to Assigned, In Route, and Complete.
  4. Open calendar details for address, asset, truck, and driver notes.
  5. Open Inspection to finish the driver checklist and closeout package.

Inspection master-detail

  1. Open Inspection. The left list shows multiple inspection jobs from public/data/inspections.json.
  2. Select a row to load the detail pane for that job, customer, container, and address.
  3. Work the same five checklist steps for every job: empty/access, photos, condition, placement or pickup notes, and signature.
  4. Attach placement photos and capture the customer signature in the demo slots.
  5. Add a completion note, then Complete inspection. Progress and notes save through /api/admin/inspections.

Demo note: the dispatch board still uses local UI state for lane movement. Inspection checklist progress, photos, signature flags, and completion notes persist to the inspections JSON file.
