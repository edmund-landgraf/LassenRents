Dispatch and driver flow

Dispatch turns rentals and pickups into scheduled jobs with the right truck, driver, address, notes, and status.

Operational flow

  1. Review unassigned dropoff, pickup, and service jobs.
  2. Match the job to truck capability such as 20 ft, 40 ft, and high-cube hauling.
  3. Advance the job from Unassigned to Assigned, In Route, and Complete.
  4. Open calendar details for address, asset, truck, and driver notes.
  5. Complete the driver inspection checklist before closeout.

Demo note: board movement is local UI state so the workflow can be shown without a database.
