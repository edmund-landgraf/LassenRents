Customer portal

Contracts, balances, and payments

The portal gives customers a simple view of active rentals, open balances, payment history, contracts, and pickup requests.

What customers can do

  1. Log in with the test client button.
  2. Review the current rental, balance, and next due date.
  3. Open contracts and preview the generated PDF rental agreement.
  4. Use the payment page to submit a stubbed demo card payment.
  5. Request pickup when they are ready to end the rental.

Demo note: payment processing is intentionally stubbed and does not send funds or save real card data.
