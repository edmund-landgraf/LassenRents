Admin backend

Admin operations overview

The backend prototype ties together the office work that happens before, during, and after a rental.

Primary modules

  1. Leads: review quote submissions and match them to inventory.
  2. Customers: view account details, balances, rentals, notes, and contact actions.
  3. New Rental: convert a lead into terms, agreement, invoice, and delivery job.
  4. Inventory and Trucks: maintain units and dispatch capability.
  5. Invoices, Activity, Calendar, and Tax Export: keep office work visible.

Demo note: login uses the test admin button and stores authentication in browser local storage.
