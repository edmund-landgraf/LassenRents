Ending a rental

Closeout connects the customer pickup request to dispatch, driver inspection, final invoice adjustments, and deposit return.

Closeout steps

  1. Customer requests pickup with preferred date, phone, and site access notes.
  2. Office or dispatch schedules the pickup and assigns the proper truck.
  3. Driver completes the inspection checklist and attaches placement or pickup photos.
  4. Office reviews damage, cleaning, rent, pickup, and adjustment items.
  5. Rental is closed and any deposit refund is staged.

Demo note: workflow steps are static markdown files in public/workflows/end-rental.
