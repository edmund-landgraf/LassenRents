# Inspect container for damages

Lassen Rents inspects the returned container for damage, missing parts, cleaning needs, and ordinary wear.

- Photograph all sides, doors, floor, lockbox, vents, and any accessories.
- Compare condition against the outbound delivery condition.
- Record damage, cleaning, missing lockbox, or accessory issues.
- Estimate repair charges when damage is beyond ordinary wear and tear.
- If no damage is found, approve the deposit for refund.
