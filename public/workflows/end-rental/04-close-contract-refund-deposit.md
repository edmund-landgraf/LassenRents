# Close contract and refund deposit

After inspection, close the rental agreement and settle the final balance.

- Finalize unpaid rent, pickup fee, damage charges, cleaning fees, credits, and taxes.
- Close the rental agreement when no further charges remain.
- Refund the deposit when there is no damage or after approved deductions.
- Send the final invoice or receipt to the client.
- Mark the container available, maintenance, or repair depending on inspection results.
